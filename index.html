Travelease: A website for Tours & Travels Business

Travelease is an all-in-one travel platform designed to simplify your journey from planning to booking. It features curated destinations—ranging from serene landscapes to adventurous escapes—with tour cards that include images, details, pricing, duration, and an easy reserve button. Behind the scenes, a secure admin dashboard allows authorized users to manage travel listings by adding, editing, or removing packages, keeping the platform updated and reliable for every traveler.

"Hills & Horizon: A Minimalist, Media-Focused Website for a Creative Production House"

Hills & Horizon's website showcases a minimalist, visually-driven design perfect for a production house. The layout emphasizes full-bleed media, with a clean color scheme and bold typography keeping focus on their portfolio. Smooth navigation, hover effects, and responsive grids enhance UX, while subtle animations add polish. Though effective, stronger CTAs and project case studies could further boost engagement. The site successfully merges aesthetics with functionality, reflecting professional creative standards.
